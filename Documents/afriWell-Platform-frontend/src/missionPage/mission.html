    Our mission is to improve mental health care solutions by enhancing the psychological well-being of an entire community, using our skills and experience to reduce the number of people that are affected by mental health issues.

    About Us

    The front is the project that has the greatest impact on society. It was established in 2019 after seeing the negative impact mental health problems have on society. According to the statistics made by worldwide mental health shows that over 970 million people in the world are affected by a mental disorder but only 2 % of the overall budget goes for mental health treatment. Honestly, it is a sad story because today's generations are the ones that are affected the most and they are dying slowly because they are ashamed of talking about what is happening to their mind. After making special research on mental health problems, we have found that online counseling is one of the greatest solutions because users will feel comfortable and confident when they talk to a counselor that's why the front is here. We have psychologists and experts that can help you to calm your situation.
